Tuttifonia

Tuttifonia interacts and responds to rhythmic inputs players play. Pressing any number key will "play" the instrument currently selected. When one stops, the computer responds. Pressing space key, will change the instrument. (Tuttifonia was conceived to be used in portuguese laptop Magalhães.)
